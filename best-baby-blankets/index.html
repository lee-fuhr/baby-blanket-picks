Quick answer

The Aden & Anais Classic Swaddle 4-Pack is our top pick for its soft, breathable muslin that works as a swaddle, stroller cover, and nursing blanket, backed by countless parent reviews.

The SwaddleMe Original is the most popular choice for quick, secure wrapping, while the Copper Pearl offers a luxe, oversized option; the Burt's Bees Beekeeper is a standout for organic cotton, and the Miracle Blanket is a runner-up for babies who break free from standard swaddles.

Baby blankets are one of the most versatile items in your newborn arsenal. They swaddle for sleep, shield from the sun, double as a burp cloth, and provide a clean surface for tummy time. But with so many options, the choice comes down to fabric, size, and how well they hold a swaddle without overheating. Start with material: lightweight muslin is breathable and flexible for swaddling, while thicker cotton or fleece suits cooler climates or older babies. Size matters too, an extra-large blanket gives you more tucking options, but a too-big blanket can be unwieldy for new parents. Look for generous dimensions (most swaddle blankets measure 40 to 48 inches square) and easy-to-follow folds. And don’t forget multipurpose use: a blanket that works as a stroller cover or play mat saves space in your diaper bag.
