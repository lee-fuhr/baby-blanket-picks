Quick answer

For most families seeking a budget-friendly baby blanket that does double duty as a swaddle and a receiving blanket, the Burt's Bees Baby Unisex Beekeeper Swaddle Blanket is our top pick thanks to its soft, breathable muslin and generous size.

The Miracle Blanket Swaddle is a runner-up for parents who need a foolproof, escape-proof swaddle, while the Hudson Baby Unisex Baby Flannel Receiving Blankets, 4-Pack is the best choice for those who want a stack of cozy, low-cost blankets for everyday messes.

Newborns spend a lot of time wrapped up, so a good blanket isn’t just about warmth, it affects their sleep quality and your peace of mind. Budget-friendly options abound, but the trick is finding one that’s soft enough for delicate skin, breathable to reduce overheating, and big enough to create a secure swaddle or a clean surface for diaper changes. When choosing a budget baby blanket, consider the material (muslin is lightweight and breathable; flannel is warmer and softer), the size (larger squares make swaddling easier), and the number of blankets you get. A single versatile swaddle might be all you need if you plan to use it mostly for sleep, while a multi-pack is better for parents who want extras for the diaper bag, car seat, and spit-up emergencies.
